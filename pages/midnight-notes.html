Midnight Notes

A calm-first journaling experience that blends tactile motion, local-first privacy, and AI prompts that feel like a coach instead of a chatbot.

Focus, reflect, and ship better ideas

Midnight Notes began as a personal ritual for end-of-day retros. It evolved into a micro interaction system that reacts to breathing cadence, surfaces memory cues, and syncs nothing to the cloud unless users explicitly publish.

Request full case study

Role

Product design · Research · Prototyping

Timeline

Mar 2024 — Present

Stack

SwiftUI · CoreML · CloudKit

Why it exists

Professionals needed a ritual that felt like a studio, not a productivity app.

  • Audio-reactive gradients mirror the user’s pace.
  • Writing sessions lock distractions and send nudges to dim lights.
  • Prompts adapt using semantic clustering of previous entries.

Key modules

  • Timeline heatmap showing streaks, mood, and “aha” density.
  • On-device embedding index for instant semantic search.
  • Story export engine that formats highlights for newsletters.

What’s next

  • Private beta with therapists to co-design reflection blueprints.
  • Integrations with Notion and Apple Shortcuts for handoff.
  • Launch kit with pricing experiments and waitlist automation.
