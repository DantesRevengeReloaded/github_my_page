Studio Atmos

Immersive, generative visuals for retail and gallery spaces that respond to audio, scent cues, and live crowd data.

Turn any venue into a living canvas

Studio Atmos streams shader compositions that morph with BPM, color palettes set by merch drops, and micro sensors detecting dwell time. It’s a kinetic storytelling layer for physical spaces.

Role

Creative coding · Technical direction

Timeline

Jan 2023 — Oct 2023

Stack

TouchDesigner · WebGL · ESP32 sensors

Experience loop

  • Audio fingerprinting aligns generative patterns with playlists.
  • Heat-mapping tracks traffic and shifts palettes to guide flow.
  • Operators schedule “chapters” that blend visual scenes with product drops.

Toolkit

  • Custom shader editor with presets for art directors.
  • Edge devices that render locally to avoid latency.
  • Remote health dashboard for sensors and projectors.

Deployments

  • Flagship pop-up for an athleisure brand in Berlin.
  • Gallery residency where visitors co-created visuals.
  • Concept store integration tied to scent diffusers.
